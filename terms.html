1. What InboxAI does

InboxAI connects to your Gmail account, with your permission, to classify, label, and optionally archive emails based on automated rules. It only acts when you click Scan or Clean up — it does not run in the background, send emails on your behalf, or delete anything permanently.

2. Free vs Premium

  • Free: 5 scans per day, up to 50 emails per scan, smart labels, basic phishing detection.
  • Premium: Unlimited scans, up to 200 emails per scan, bulk cleanup of 500+ emails at once, AI-generated explanations for flagged emails, advanced inbox analytics.
