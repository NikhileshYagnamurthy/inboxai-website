Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

InboxAI ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. What Data We Collect

  • Gmail Email Address: Used for authentication and to identify your account.
  • Email Metadata: Subject, sender, and snippet of emails — only when you click "Scan".
  • No Email Content Stored: We do not store the full content of your emails.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To classify and organize your emails into smart labels (Promotions, Newsletters, Security, etc.).
  • To provide inbox analytics (clutter score, phishing count, etc.).
  • To improve our AI classification engine.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Google OAuth: For authentication and Gmail API access.
  • Razorpay: For processing payments.

4. Data Security

  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS.
  • Your Gmail token is stored securely on our server.
  • We never share your data with third parties.
  • We do not store your email content permanently.

5. Your Rights

  • You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account.
  • You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.
